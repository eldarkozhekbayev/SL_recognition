Learn ASL / BSL Fingerspelling

This WeCapable Tool takes the English text from you and plays the converted fingerspelling. Thus, it helps you in learning ASL and practicing your knowledge of the same.

You have the option of controlling the speed of playback. More learned people may want to speed up the playback and still recognize the fingerspelled words. Beginners may want to see the playback at lower speeds. You can also mirror the hand -- sometimes it helps you to practice better. You can change the size of the hand and also opt for the dark mode to reduce the screen glare.
